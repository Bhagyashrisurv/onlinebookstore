SHIPMENTS AND RETURNS


Shipment:

Packages are typically shipped within 1 business day (M-F) after receipt of payment and are shipped via USPS with tracking. Standard shipping typically takes 5-10 days and Expedited shipping 2-5 days. Shipping costs will be shown during checkout.

Returns:

Not happy with your purchase? No Problem, we are happy to accept returns within 30 days of your purchase for a full refund. We stand behind every item we sell and if you are not happy we will make it right! All returns must be postmarked within 30 days of purchase.

The fine print on returns:

To preserve a safe, inclusive, healthy environment for all customers, we limit each account/shipping address to a limit of 5 full refunds per month. Any returns after 5 will be accepted/processed at our discretion. We reserve the right to restrict, suspend, and/or discontinue the participation of any member whose program behavior is deemed to be abusive, offensive, inappropriate or objectionable.
